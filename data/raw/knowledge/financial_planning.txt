Financial planning is the process of managing income, expenses, savings, and investments.

Core components:
1. Income management
2. Expense tracking
3. Savings planning
4. Investment strategy

Rules:
- Savings should be at least 20% of income
- Expenses should not exceed 50–60% of income
- Financial plans must adapt to changes in income and lifestyle

Goal Types:
- Short-term: emergency fund, small purchases
- Medium-term: car, travel, education
- Long-term: retirement, wealth creation

A good financial plan:
- Prioritizes savings before spending
- Avoids unnecessary debt
- Balances risk and returns

Without planning:
→ High probability of overspending and low savings
