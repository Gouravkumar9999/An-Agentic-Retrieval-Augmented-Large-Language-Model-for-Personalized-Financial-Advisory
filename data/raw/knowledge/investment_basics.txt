Investment allocation based on risk:

Low risk:
- 60% bonds
- 30% index funds
- 10% cash

Medium risk:
- 40% index funds
- 30% stocks
- 20% bonds
- 10% cash

High risk:
- 70% stocks
- 20% index funds
- 10% alternative assets

During high inflation:
- Reduce cash holdings
- Invest in inflation-protected assets (TIPS, gold, real estate)

Long-term investing (>5 years) generally outperforms short-term trading.

Avoid investing if emergency fund is not established.
