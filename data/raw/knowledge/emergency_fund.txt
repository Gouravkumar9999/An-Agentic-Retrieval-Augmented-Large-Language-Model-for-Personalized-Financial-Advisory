Emergency fund is essential for financial stability.

It should cover:
- Minimum: 3 months of expenses
- Ideal: 6 months of expenses

Formula:
Emergency Fund = Monthly Expenses × 3 to 6

Rules:
- Must be easily accessible (savings account / liquid fund)
- Should NOT be invested in high-risk assets
- Should be used only for emergencies (job loss, medical, crisis)

If emergency fund = 0:
→ Financial risk is HIGH

If emergency fund < 3 months:
→ Financial stability is WEAK

Building emergency fund is the FIRST priority before investing.
